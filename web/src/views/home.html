Create your own games!

Codako was inspired by the KidSIM research of the 1990's,
and is targeted at young learners ages 6-12.

Create Worlds

Use Codako's built-in drawing tools to create characters and place them into your game world. You can create mario-style adventure games, simulations, zelda-style exploration games, and more!

Learn Core Concepts

Codako introduces many programming concepts, without young learners needing to write actual code. It lays a foundation of understanding that students can carry with them as they learn to program.

View details »

Research Based Approach

Codako is heavily inspired by the KidSIM research conducted by Apple's Advanced Technologies Group in the late 1990s, which showed that students were very successful in programming-by-demonstration environments. As Codako is developed, several more studies will be conducted around it's effectiveness.

View details »

Advanced Features

Codako was designed to be simple, and students can learn more about advanced features as they need them. It supports variables, characters with multiple appearances, event-triggered rules, rule containers that teach control flow, and more!

View details »

Built with love

Codako will always be free to play, and will never collect information beyond the required email address, nickname and password. 20 years ago, I learned to program using Apple's KidSIM, and Codako is my effort to bring back the platform that inspired me in my childhood.
